About GamePad Tester

We're passionate gamers dedicated to helping you get the most out of your gaming controllers. Our mission is to provide the best free testing tools for the gaming community.

Our Story

GamePad Tester was born out of frustration. As avid gamers, we've all experienced that moment when you're not sure if it's your controller that's the problem or if you're just having an off day. Stick drift, unresponsive buttons, and calibration issues can make even the best players look like beginners.

We realized there was a need for a simple, reliable, and free tool that could instantly diagnose controller issues. No downloads, no complicated setup, no technical jargon – just connect your controller and get answers.

Since launching, we've helped over 100,000 gamers worldwide test their controllers and get back to what they love most – gaming without frustration.

Our Mission

To empower gamers worldwide with the tools they need to maintain their gaming equipment and enjoy uninterrupted gaming experiences. We believe that technical issues shouldn't stand between you and your passion for gaming.

What We Do

We provide comprehensive, browser-based testing tools for gaming controllers that work with PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo, and PC gamepads. Our tools can detect:

  • Stick Drift: Unwanted movement in analog sticks
  • Button Response: Dead or sticky buttons
  • Trigger Sensitivity: Calibration and range issues
  • Vibration Function: Haptic feedback problems
  • Connection Issues: Wireless and wired connectivity
