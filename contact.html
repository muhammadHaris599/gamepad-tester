Quick Help

Before reaching out, check these common questions that might help you immediately:

Why isn't my controller being detected? +

Make sure your controller is properly connected via USB or Bluetooth, press any button to activate it, and ensure your browser supports gamepad input (Chrome, Firefox, Edge work best).

The stick drift test shows false positives. What should I do? +

Ensure the controller is on a stable surface and not being touched. Some controllers have natural variance. If the issue persists, try calibrating your controller in your system settings first.

Can I test multiple controllers at once? +

Yes! Our tool supports up to 4 controllers simultaneously. Simply connect them and select which one you want to test from the controller selection panel.

Is my data safe when using the tool? +

Absolutely! Everything runs locally in your browser. We don't collect or store any personal data or controller information. Your privacy is our priority.
