Specialized Controller Market 2025

The specialized gaming controller market has matured significantly in 2025, with established brands like Thrustmaster, Logitech, and Hori focusing on specific gaming niches and user needs. Unlike general-purpose controllers, these manufacturers have developed expertise in particular areas: Thrustmaster in racing and modular design, Logitech in PC gaming and affordability, and Hori in fighting games and arcade-style controllers.

As gaming controller specialists, we've extensively tested the flagship offerings from these three distinctive manufacturers: the Thrustmaster eSwap X Pro with its revolutionary modular design, the Logitech F710 representing reliable PC gaming, and the Hori Fighting Commander designed specifically for competitive fighting games.

Understanding specialized controller design requires knowledge of how different gaming genres demand specific features. Our analysis of controller customization and modification provides essential background on why specialized controllers often outperform general-purpose alternatives in their target applications.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which controller is best for racing games: Thrustmaster or Logitech?

The Thrustmaster eSwap X Pro is better for racing games due to its modular design, precise analog triggers, and specialized racing game optimization. Logitech F710 is more suitable for general gaming with some racing capability.

Is the Hori Fighting Commander worth it for fighting games?

Yes, the Hori Fighting Commander is specifically designed for fighting games with its 6-button layout, precise D-pad, and tournament-grade construction. It's the preferred choice for many professional fighting game players.

Do Thrustmaster controllers work with all gaming platforms?

Thrustmaster controllers primarily support PC and Xbox platforms. The eSwap X Pro works with Xbox Series X|S, Xbox One, and PC, but has limited support for PlayStation and Nintendo Switch.

Which brand offers the best modular controller design?

Thrustmaster leads in modular controller design with the eSwap series, allowing users to swap out sticks, D-pads, and other components. This makes it highly customizable for different gaming genres and preferences.

Is the Logitech F710 still relevant in 2025?

Yes, the Logitech F710 remains highly relevant for PC gaming due to its excellent compatibility, reliable wireless performance, and unbeatable value. It's particularly good for retro gaming and emulation.

Can I use fighting game controllers for other genres?

While possible, fighting game controllers like the Hori Fighting Commander are optimized specifically for fighting games and may not perform well in genres requiring analog stick precision, such as racing or FPS games.
